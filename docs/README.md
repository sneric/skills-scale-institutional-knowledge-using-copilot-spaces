# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

Welcome! This documentation serves as your comprehensive guide to OctoAcme's project management processes, practices, and workflows.

## Overview

OctoAcme's project management approach emphasizes iterative delivery, clear ownership, and data-informed decision making. At the core of our methodology is a customer-first mindset that prioritizes delivering value in small, testable increments. Each project follows a structured lifecycle from initiation through retrospective, ensuring consistent execution while maintaining the flexibility to adapt based on evidence and feedback. Our approach balances rigorous process discipline with psychological safety, encouraging teams to experiment, learn, and continuously improve.

Our workflows are built around well-defined roles and responsibilities. Project Managers coordinate delivery timelines, manage risks, and facilitate cross-team communication. Product Managers define outcomes, prioritize the backlog, and measure success through clear metrics. Delivery Managers optimize team processes and remove organizational blockers. Quality Assurance Leads establish testing strategies and quality standards. Change Champions drive adoption of new practices and tools. Communications Liaisons ensure transparent stakeholder engagement. Developers and other specialists collaborate throughout the lifecycle to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards. This clear role delineation, documented in our Roles & Responsibilities Matrix, ensures accountability while fostering collaboration across functional boundaries.

Communication is central to OctoAcme's project success. We maintain a regular cadence of weekly syncs between project and product leads, twice-weekly team standups, and monthly stakeholder updates. Risk management is proactive and ongoing, with teams maintaining risk registers that are reviewed and updated at each sync. Our communication templates and escalation paths ensure transparency and enable rapid response when issues arise. Whether it's a routine status update or an incident requiring immediate attention, our structured communication strategies keep all stakeholders informed and aligned.

Quality assurance and continuous improvement are woven throughout our entire process. Teams define clear acceptance criteria and definitions of done at the planning stage, with QA validating against these standards during execution. After each release, retrospectives capture learnings and generate actionable items for the next iteration. This commitment to reflection and adaptation, combined with our emphasis on test coverage, observability, and maintainable code, ensures that OctoAcme projects not only deliver on their commitments but also contribute to long-term product quality and team effectiveness.

## Getting Started

If you're new to OctoAcme project management:

1. Start with the [Project Management Overview](./octoacme-project-management-overview.md) to understand our core principles and approach
2. Review [Roles and Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) to understand your role and how you fit into the team
3. Check the [Roles & Responsibilities Matrix](./octoacme-roles-matrix.md) to see who you'll collaborate with and on what activities
4. Use the [Team Onboarding Guide](./octoacme-onboarding-guide.md) template to structure your first 90 days
5. Refer to the specific process documents (Initiation, Planning, Execution, etc.) as you work through each phase of your project
