## Week 1: Get Oriented

### Day 1-2: Access & Setup
- [ ] Complete IT onboarding and access requests
- [ ] Join team communication channels (Slack/Teams/etc.)
- [ ] Set up development environment (if applicable)
- [ ] Review project repository and documentation
- [ ] Schedule 1:1 introductions with key team members

### Day 3-5: Learn the Context
- [ ] Read the Project Charter and One-pager
- [ ] Review the [OctoAcme Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Read your role description in [OctoAcme Roles & Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)
- [ ] Review the [Roles & Responsibilities Matrix](octoacme-roles-matrix.md)
- [ ] Attend daily standup and weekly sync meetings
- [ ] Ask questions! Set up time with your manager or buddy

### End of Week 1: Check-in
- [ ] 1:1 with your manager to review progress and questions
- [ ] Identify any access or tooling gaps
- [ ] Set goals for Week 2

---

## Week 2-4: Start Contributing

### Week 2: Shadow & Learn
- [ ] Shadow team members in your role or similar roles
- [ ] Participate in sprint planning or project meetings
- [ ] Review recent project artifacts (PRs, test reports, status updates)
- [ ] Take on your first small task or contribution
- [ ] Continue attending all regular team meetings

### Week 3: Increase Responsibility
- [ ] Take on 2-3 tasks independently with support
- [ ] Start participating actively in standups and planning
- [ ] Begin regular responsibilities (e.g., status updates, testing, reviews)
- [ ] Provide feedback on onboarding process

### Week 4: Full Speed
- [ ] Carry regular workload for your role
- [ ] Establish working rhythms with key collaborators
- [ ] Identify one process improvement or documentation gap
- [ ] 1:1 with manager to review 30-day progress

## Appendix: First 90 Days

### Month 1: Foundation
- Learn the project, team, and your role
- Build relationships with key collaborators
- Start contributing to well-defined tasks
- Provide onboarding feedback

### Month 2: Growing Impact
- Take on more complex work independently
- Begin leading specific activities in your role
- Identify improvement opportunities
- Deepen technical or domain knowledge

### Month 3: Full Contributor
- Operate at full capacity in your role
- Mentor other new team members
- Drive improvements and innovations
- Set longer-term goals with your manager
